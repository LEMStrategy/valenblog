1
Go to Blogger and sign in

Blogspot is powered by Blogger — Google's free blogging platform. You only need a Google account (Gmail). No new account creation required.

→ Go to Blogger.com Create Google Account
💡
Already have Gmail? You're already signed up. Just go to Blogger.com and click "Create your blog".
2
Create a new blog

Once signed in, click "Create your blog" (or the + New Blog button if you already have one). You'll be asked to:

  • Enter a Display Name — this is your author name
  • Give your blog a Title — e.g. "My Travel Journal"
  • Choose a URL — e.g. mytravelblog.blogspot.com
ℹ
Your blog address will be yourname.blogspot.com for free. You can connect a custom domain (like yourname.com) later in settings.
3
Pick a theme & customize

From your Blogger dashboard, click Theme in the left sidebar. Choose a free theme or click Customize to change fonts, colors, and layout without coding.

🎨
Recommended themes for article-style blogs: Contempo, Soho, or Emporio — clean, readable, and mobile-friendly.
4
Write your article in Article Studio

Open the Article Studio editor, write your article in Markdown. The live preview on the right shows exactly how your content looks formatted. Use headings, bold, lists, blockquotes — all will convert perfectly.

✍ Open Article Studio
💡
Write freely in Markdown — don't worry about formatting. The export step will produce clean HTML automatically.
5
Export as HTML from Article Studio

Once your article is ready, click the Export button in the editor header (or press Ctrl+E). Then:

  1. Switch to the HTML tab in the export dialog
  2. Click "Copy to Clipboard"
<!-- Example of exported HTML from Article Studio --> <h1>My First Blog Post</h1> <p>This is my intro paragraph...</p> <h2>A Section Title</h2> <blockquote>An inspiring quote here.</blockquote>
6
Paste into Blogger & Publish

In your Blogger dashboard, click New Post. The Blogger editor has two modes — you need to switch to HTML mode:

  1. In the post editor, click the pencil icon (top-left of editor) → select "HTML view"
  2. Paste the copied HTML (Ctrl+V)
  3. Switch back to "Compose view" to preview
  4. Add a post title, set labels (tags), and add a featured image
  5. Click "Publish" — you're live!
→ Create New Blogger Post
ℹ
Tip: After pasting HTML in Blogger, switch to Compose view to check formatting visually before publishing. You can also schedule posts for a future date.
7
Optional: Connect a Custom Domain

Upgrade your blog from yourname.blogspot.com to yourname.com for a more professional look. You'll need to purchase a domain from a registrar (Namecheap, Google Domains, GoDaddy).

  1. In Blogger: go to Settings → Publishing → Custom domain
  2. Enter your domain name
  3. Blogger shows you CNAME records to add to your domain's DNS
  4. Add those records in your domain registrar's DNS settings
  5. Wait 10–60 minutes for propagation
💡
Domains typically cost $10–15/year. Blogger itself remains completely free even with a custom domain.
